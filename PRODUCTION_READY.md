## Design System

### Color System
- **Primary**: Orange (actions, focus)
- **Accent**: Green (success, completed)
- **Neutral**: Grays (backgrounds, text, borders)
- **No gradients** anywhere

### Typography
- **Headings**: h1-h6 normalized (36px to 16px)
- **Body**: 16px, relaxed line-height
- **Font**: Geist sans-serif

### Spacing
- **8px grid**: All spacing in multiples of 8px
- **Utilities**: spacing-xs through spacing-2xl
- **Touch targets**: 44px minimum (accessible)

### Components
- **Buttons**: Primary (size="lg"), Secondary (outline), Tertiary (ghost)
- **Cards**: Consistent 24px padding (16px mobile)
- **Hierarchy**: PRIMARY (focus), SECONDARY (context), TERTIARY (background)
